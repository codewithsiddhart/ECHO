// ABOUT
ECHO
a rhythm that learns you... then betrays you.
MODES
Easy → Medium → Hard → Impossible. Each has its own timing windows, speed, and win condition. Your best streak is saved per mode.
GHOST
Your best run is recorded and replayed as a ghost on the leaderboard — you compete against yourself.
REPLAY
After each run, the end screen shows a waveform of your accuracy — perfect (cyan), good (blue), miss (pink).
